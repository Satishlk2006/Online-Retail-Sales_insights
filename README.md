# 🔍 Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

- Total revenue generated is **$10.6 million**.
- Sales remain strongest during **September through November**, indicating a clear seasonal increase in demand.

### Geographic Distribution

- Domestic customers contribute approximately **90%** of total sales.
- International customers account for approximately **10%** of total sales.
- Sales are distributed across **38 countries**, demonstrating an international customer base while remaining primarily domestic.

### Product Performance

The highest revenue-generating products include:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these three products contribute approximately **47% of total revenue**, making them significant drivers of overall sales.

### Invoice Analysis

- A total of **19,960 invoices** were analyzed.
- The average invoice value is approximately **$533.171**, providing an overview of typical transaction size.

### Monthly Trend

Sales show noticeable growth leading into the final quarter of the year.

**Note:** The dataset includes **December 2011**, but the available records for that month may be incomplete. Therefore, any decline observed during December should not be interpreted as a confirmed decrease in business performance without additional data.
